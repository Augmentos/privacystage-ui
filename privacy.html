Privacy Policy

Last updated:

PrivacyStage is built around a simple principle: your screen is yours. This policy explains exactly what data exists as a result of using or buying PrivacyStage, why, and where it lives. There's less here than in most software privacy policies, because the app itself doesn't send your screen, your windows, or your usage anywhere.

1. What the app does locally (nothing leaves your Mac)

PrivacyStage requests macOS Screen Recording permission to capture the windows you tag and render them onto a virtual display, which you then share yourself in a video call. This capture and rendering happens entirely on your Mac. We do not receive, store, or have any access to your screen contents, window contents, tags, or usage of the app. There is no telemetry or analytics in the app itself.

3. What we don't do

  • No tracking pixels, no cookies, no cross-site tracking. This website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics, which counts page views and visits without cookies and without collecting anything that identifies you personally.
  • No analytics of any kind inside the app itself. The app never phones home.
  • No selling, renting, or sharing your data with advertisers
  • No account system — there's nothing to create, and nothing tied to an identity beyond your license key

4. How long we keep it

License and device-activation records are kept for as long as your license is active, so we can help if you need to move it to a new Mac or have a billing question. If you'd like your record deleted, email us and we'll remove it — note this will deactivate your license, since the app checks against that record to enforce the one-device limit.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data (for example, under GDPR if you're in the EU/UK, or CCPA if you're a California resident). Contact us at the email below for any of these requests — since we collect very little data to begin with, most requests are quick to fulfill.

6. Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date above.
