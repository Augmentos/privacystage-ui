Questions

Good to know

What exactly gets shared?

Only the windows you tag to a monitor. PrivacyStage captures those windows and paints the active one onto a hidden virtual display; you share that display in your call. Untagged windows, notifications, and background apps never appear.

Will it move or resize my real windows?

No. Your windows stay exactly where they are. PrivacyStage only captures them. Nothing is moved, resized, or sent to another desktop.

How does the free trial work?

You get 3 days with every feature unlocked. Trial start is stored in your Keychain, so it survives reinstalls. When it ends, enter a license key to keep going.

How do I get my license key after paying?

Checkout is handled by Paddle. The moment payment clears, your license key is generated and emailed to you. Open PrivacyStage, paste the key, and you're unlocked. No account required.

Which Macs are supported?

macOS 14 (Sonoma) or later, on both Apple Silicon and Intel. It's a single download that runs natively on either, with no separate versions and no security warnings.

Which call apps does it work with?

Any app that can share a screen, including Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and Zoom. In the call's Share Screen dialog, pick the display named after your PrivacyStage monitor.
